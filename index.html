Instructions:
  1. Wait for data to download (takes between 20s and 2mins depending on internet speed (70mB download)). You will know it is done when AFTER clicking submit the search boxes start populating below with names
  2. Enter a USATT player name in either box and press enter or click submit button. (Name must be any substring (starting from first letter) of first OR last name (not both) OR EXACT first AND last name with one space between)-Capitalization does not matter
  3. After names populate, click on one to select and see all their match information
  4. Enjoy the graphs and then click see ALL matches to get a fully populated list of all matches played by that player
  5. Filter matches by year or wins/losses and see the person whom you played the most in any category as well as all matches
  6. Enter another name in other box (should be someone that the first person has played). After selecting the name in the list that populates, the middle section of site will show head to head matches and match score between player 1 and player 2
